Students Are Required To Take An Entrance Examination Before They Can Enroll To Their Desired Courses

Here are the guidelines for new and transferee students that is willing to enroll in Eastern Samar State University

Who are required to take the University Entrance Examination?

☑️An incoming first year student

☑️ A transfer student who have not satisfied all academic requirements
for the first year in his/her previous college/university

You may message 0951 484 3389 to set for an appointment.
Your text message should contain the following information:

☑️ Purpose (ex. application for entrance examination schedule)

☑️ Complete name

☑️ Home Address

☑️ Current School
What to bring on examination day:

☑️ Exam permit

☑️ School ID

☑️ Monggol 2 pencil

☑️ Blue ballpen

☑️ Snack (optional)

* A confirmation message indicating your schedule will be sent to you.

* On your scheduled date of application, submit the required documents together with the Application Form (to be filled-up in the venue) at the Admission office located at 1st floor, ICTC Building, ESSU Main, Brgy. Maypangdan, Borongan City. Bring your own blue ballpen for filling-up forms.

* After evaluating your documents, an Exam Permit will be issued to you indicating your schedule for Entrance Examination and other information that is deemed necessary.
